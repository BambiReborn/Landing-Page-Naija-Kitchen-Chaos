# Naija Kitchen Chaos — Landing Page

Official landing page for **Naija Kitchen Chaos**, a fast-paced multiplayer cooking game rooted in Nigerian culture, street food chaos, and the spirit of the bukka.

## About The Game

Naija Kitchen Chaos throws players into the heart of Nigeria's most chaotic kitchens — from roadside bukkas to bustling street food stalls. Cook authentic Nigerian dishes like Jollof rice, Egusi soup and Suya, communicate with your team, and survive increasingly difficult kitchens inspired by the energy and culture of Nigerian street food.

Team up with friends, cook alongside an AI sous-chef, and survive increasingly difficult kitchens inspired by the energy and culture of Nigerian street food.

## About This Project

This is the official promotional landing page for Naija Kitchen Chaos. Built to introduce the game, showcase its features and characters, and drive players to download or play.

## Built With

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript

## Sections

- **Navbar** — Logo, navigation links and Play Now button
- **Hero** — Main headline, tagline and call to action
- **About** — What is Naija Kitchen Chaos?
- **Game Features** — Authentic dishes, local multiplayer, AI sous-chef, difficult kitchens
- **Meet The Chefs** — Character showcase (Mama Bisi, Chinedu, Amina, Tunde)
- **CTA** — Ready to survive the kitchen?
- **Footer** — Quick links, support, social media and newsletter signup

## Getting Started

No installation needed. Just open `index.html` in your browser.
